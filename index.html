PAWsitive Difference is an animal rescue located in Las Vegas, NV. We rescue and find homes for as many dogs and cats as possible!

See Adoptable Pets

Our Mission

The primary goal of PAWsitive Difference is to rescue, foster, and adopt out dogs and cats from families that are unable to continue to care for them. We also rescue many pets that have been abandoned or are listed on the kill list of our local animal foundation and help provide these pets homes with new, loving families.


We took in 130 cats and 802 dogs in 2025. Help us save more in 2026!
